## Better first-run setup and Spanish transcription

OpenWispr now handles macOS permission setup more reliably after reinstalling
or resetting the app, so onboarding should stop looping once the required
permissions are granted.

This update also improves Parakeet transcription for Spanish and bilingual
dictation, preserving Spanish output instead of over-correcting it toward
English.

## Smaller fixes

- Shortened the lingering edit button delay after transcription.
- Improved model selection behavior during onboarding.
