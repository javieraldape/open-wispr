## Welcome to OpenWispr

OpenWispr is private, offline dictation for your Mac. Press your shortcut,
speak, and your words are transcribed locally and typed straight into whatever
app you're using. Nothing is sent to the cloud — all transcription happens on
your machine.

## What you get

- **Fully offline.** Your audio never leaves your computer.
- **Live streaming transcription.** See your words appear as you speak with
  compatible streaming models — try Parakeet for English or Nemotron Streaming
  for multilingual.
- **Learns your corrections.** OpenWispr picks up the names, terms, and
  spellings you fix, so it gets them right next time.

## Heads up — this is an early build

This first release isn't code-signed yet, so macOS will warn you the first time
you open it. Right-click the app and choose **Open** (you only need to do this
once). Signing and notarization are coming in a future release.
